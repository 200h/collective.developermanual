Sessions are served-side data stored by per user basis.
Sessions are available for logged in and anonymous users. 
Session is identified by a cookie. 

Setting a session parameter
---------------------------

Plone has a tool called session_data_manager. 

Example::

   sdm = self.context.session_data_manager
   session = sdm.getSessionData(create=True)
   session.set("my_option", any_python_object_supporting_pickling)
